Sunshine egg rings

Sunshine egg rings
2 red/yellow bell pepper, 2 eggs, a pinch of pepper,
a pinch of salt, 2 tablespoon oil and a piece of cilantro.

To make these delicious egg rings, follow these steps:
1. Cut 2 slices of the bell pepper, they should be about an inch thick.
2. Turn on the oven and put the pan over medium heat.
3. Pour in the oil.
4. Put in the pepper rings, and break the eggs into the rings.
5. Cook for five minutes.

Smoothie

Smoothie
1 cup of rawsberry, 1 banana and a spoon of greek yogurt.

To make this smoothie, just put everything in the blender, and wait
until it's smooth. If you want, you can also pop them in the freezer and
eat them as popsicles.

Banana cookies

Banana cookies
2 bananas, 1 teaspoon of cinnamon and 1 cup of oatmeal.

1. Squash the bananas with a fork.
2. Pour the banana in a large mixing bowl.
3. Mix in the rest of the ingredients.
4. Bake until golden brown.
